Who We Are

At India Shipping, we are dedicated to simplifying global logistics by offering end-to-end freight forwarding solutions. Our expertise in ocean freight, air freight, customs brokerage, and warehousing ensures that your cargo moves smoothly across borders with minimal delays and maximum efficiency. With years of experience in freight forwarding and a customer-first approach, India Shipping has established itself as a trusted partner in the logistics industry. Our expert team is committed to providing customized solutions, ensuring your supply chain runs smoothly from start to finish.

Our Expertise

In order to provide a comprehensive service, we have realized that it is important to have a strict control of the operations, hence under the control of the Board of Directors each of these activities are managed by senior professionals with supporting experienced staff. It is our family values that make us one strong team. As individual units our companies offer specialization, expertise, efficiency and excellence in their individual capacity but together they work as a team. We specialize in providing seamless global logistics solutions tailored to meet your specific shipping needs. As a trusted freight forwarder, we connect businesses to international markets with a comprehensive range of services including ocean freight, air freight, customs brokerage, and supply chain management.

Our Commitment

We offer our clients personalized, prompt, efficient service. Our information technology has systems support which enables us to track cargo on-line especially project cargo. This in turn improves our operative efficiency & guarantees good service. We intend to develop the companies further by abiding by our core values so that we can maintain a leading, competitive position as a provider of 'Sustainable Shipping Solutions'. Ocean Freight services include Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) services with flexible schedules and competitive pricing, Air Freight provides expedited shipping solutions for time-sensitive cargo, our Customs Brokerage ensures smooth customs clearance by managing all documentation and compliance requirements, while our Warehousing & Distribution offers secure storage facilities and efficient distribution networks to support your supply chain, complemented by Door-to-Door Services that handle every step from pickup to final delivery.
